Hotondo Homes

Details

Hotondo Homes prides itself on being a family-owned business established in 1979. It sources 70 independently owned and operated local home builders across Australia, including Victoria, Queensland, NSW, ACT, South Australia and Tasmania.

Hotondo Homes offers a variety of home building services. For example, you can design a new home after taking inspiration from one of Hotondo Homes’ display homes (or even buy a display home), invest in a house and land package, or hire Hotondo to help with a knockdown and rebuild.

Points of praise for Hotondo Homes:

  • Exceptional customer support was a recurring point of praise from reviewers. Home builders greatly appreciated that they were kept in the loop during all stages of the home building process, thanks to clear and effective communication.
  • The technical knowledge and extensive experience of home builders was top notch. The particulars of the building work – such as structural integrity and quality of materials used - was also commended by reviewers.
  • The overall cost of a home building project was often cited as affordable by reviewers.
  • First homeowners were particularly impressed and relieved at how smooth they found the overall home building process.

With its host of happy homeowners, it's no surprise that Hotondo Homes is the 2021 Award Winner for Best National Home Builder. Top scores were awarded for the company’s caring customer service, the structural integrity of houses, and timeliness when completing projects.

Top-performing offices with the highest overall ratings were Toowoomba and Mackay in Queensland, as well as the Bathurst and Orange offices in New South Wales.
